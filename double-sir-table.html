Round logic

Win two tricks in a row with a non‑Ace to capture the entire table pile.
  • 7.Trump keeper leads the very first card of the game and sets the opening suit.
  • 8.Players must follow the suit of the turn if they have it; otherwise they may discard or (after reveal) play trump.
  • 11.Trump keeper's partner without suit wastes any card face up; trump is still hidden.
  • 12.Opponent without suit forces trump reveal; from then on that suit beats all other suits.
  • 13.Trump keeper without suit discards face down; card is out of play but not visible.
  • 18.Same player must win two consecutive tricks to claim all cards currently on the table.
  • 19.An Ace cannot be the second winning card that collects the pile, but that win still counts towards the streak.
  • 20.If the second win is with an Ace, pile stays and the player must win again using a non‑Ace.
  • 21.If trump is never revealed until the last card, trump suit loses its special power and behaves like any normal suit.
Attack with trump or waste safely when off‑suit.
